## Data We Collect

- **Local data**: QR scan logs, profiles, and notes are stored locally on your device.

- **Authentication**: We use Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for authentication. We do not store your passwords.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect any personal data without your consent.
- We do not sell your data to third parties.
- We do not track your location.
