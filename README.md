## FAQ

**How do I scan a QR code?**
Tap the blue scan button on the main screen, select a category, and point your camera at a QR code.

**How do I export my logs?**
Go to Logs screen, tap the export button, and choose CSV format.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases".

**How do I backup my data?**
Upgrade to Pro, sign in with Google, and your data will sync automatically.
